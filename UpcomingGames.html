Known Upcoming Releases to the Fallout Franchise

Fallout 5

Fallout 5 is the only known and confirmed future entry to the Fallout franchise at this time. It will be developed by the current owner of the Fallout IP Bethesda Studios. Bethesda CEO Todd Howard has said that Fallout 5 development will begin after the release of the Elder Scrolls 6 which is slated for 2025. This information gives us a projected release date for Fallout being from the late 2020's to the early 2030's.

Developer: Bethesda

Release Date: Project for late 2020's to early 2030's
