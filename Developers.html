Developers of the Fallout Games over the years

Interplay

Games Developed: Fallout, Fallout: Brotherhood of Steel

Interplay were the team that started the franchise by creating the original Fallout and being involved in the development of Fallout 2 and later creating Fallout: Brotherhood of Steel before handing over the IP to Bethesda

Black Isle

Games Developed: Fallout 2

Black Isle is actually still Interplay but rather a smaller division of Interplay so not being their main development team, they had the job of giving the original Fallout a sequel and created Fallout 2

Micro Forté

Games Developed: Fallout Tactics

Micro Forté got to create a Fallout game after Fallout 2 but instead made a spin-off of the main series being Fallout: Tactics

Obsidian

Games Developed: Fallout New Vegas

Obsidian was hired by Bethesda to make another entry into the Fallout series and many people to this day still believe Fallout New Vegas is the best entry into the series

Bethesda

Games Developed: Fallout 3, Fallout 4, Fallout 76

Bethesda is the current owner of the Fallout IP after the acquired it from Interplay before they made Fallout 3 which brought the game from 2D to the 3D which changed the franchise for the future. They are also in charge of making the next main entry into the series being Fallout 5
